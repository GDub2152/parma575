Summer Picnic

Sunday, July 26, 2026
11:00 AM – 4:00 PM

James Day Park
Big Creek Pavilion
11828 West Pleasant Valley Road
Parma, Ohio 44130

Come join us for our Parma 575 Bigfoot family potluck picnic on Sunday July 26th 11:00 am to 4pm. We will eat around 12 noon at James Day park 11828 W Pleasant Valley Rd, Parma, OH, 44130 the large Pavilion in the back , it has electric and charcoal grills. We will have access to the pool for $5 for Parma residents / $6 for non-residents ( bring ID and cash and swimwear ) There will be Corn Hole, Putt Putt and a playground for the kids. We will also be giving away prizes! 1st is a 25 watt GMRS Mobile Radio. Second, third, fourth and fifth prizes are all going to be 5 watt GMRS Handheld Radios. Radio Prizes are only available to adults 18 and over. Maybe someone can provide prizes for the kids? Everyone will need to bring a dish, we will need mains, sides and desserts. We will have to find a way to decide who will bring what ( I could use some help with this please ). Please provide your own non-alcoholic beverages.
